My academic background has given me a solid foundation, and I’m enthusiastic about blending that knowledge with web development to create engaging and effective digital experiences. When I’m not coding or studying, you’ll find me exploring the latest tech trends or experimenting with small coding projects to challenge myself. I’m passionate about continuous learning and eager to grow in this ever-evolving field. Thanks for stopping by to learn a bit about me!

Why Web design?

I chose web design because it perfectly blends my passion for technology with creativity. As an Engineer, I’ve always been fascinated by how technology shapes our world. Web design allows me to bring ideas to life by creating websites that are not only visually appealing but also highly functional and user-friendly. It’s exciting to solve problems through design and to continually learn new skills and tools as the digital landscape evolves. Pursuing web design feels like the right path to combine my technical foundation with creative expression, enabling me to build engaging digital experiences that truly connect with people.
